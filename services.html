Our Services

Play Africa offers a range of educational programmes and experiences designed to develop children's skills through play and discovery.


Programme Description Who It's For
Children's Planetarium Explore the galaxy through a state-of-the-art planetarium experience. Learners discover planets, stars, and the solar system in an immersive dome setting. Schools & Families
Earthwise World An environment education programme that teaches children about nature, sustainability, and how to care for our planet. Communities come together to inspire the next generation. All Ages
Science Film Festival An annual festival that makes science fun and accessible through engaging films. It reaches over 500,000 children worldwide each year and is free for participating schools. School groups
Field Trip Programme Structure and curriculum-aligned school field trips to Constitution Hill. Learners explore history, science, and arts through guided activities and exhibits. Schools
Interactive Exhibits Permanent and rotating hands-on exhibits that develop creativity, problem solving, critical thinking, and collaboration skills in a fun environment. Children & Families
